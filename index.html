ABOUT

This App is borne out of the need many people are currently facing due to the pandemic. Many have learning objectives that they are unable to fulfill because there is now more to do, an approach to learning is exposure to new things. With this method, subjects of interests are broken down small learning objectives and each of these objects are outlined in short articles, the users read the articles in a sequence and then answer some short quiz to demonstrate/evaluate their understanding.

This approach works because the articles (knowledge) and the quiz (assessment) are bundled in on the same application, the articles are also small enough that the reader is able to read in a few minutes, and the questions in the quiz are structured such that they expose/highlight the important points in the subject matter
